Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how the "Am I Covered?" lead education tool ("we," "us," or "our") collects, uses, and shares information about you when you use our website.

Information We Collect

When you submit the contact form, we collect:

  • Your first name
  • Your phone number
  • Your zip code and inferred state
  • Your responses to the scenarios and questions presented
  • How you arrived at our site (UTM parameters, referral source)
  • The date and time of your submission

How We Use Your Information

The information you provide is used solely to:

  • Connect you with a licensed insurance professional in your state who can review your coverage
  • Contact you by phone or text message regarding insurance coverage options
  • Improve our educational content and user experience

TCPA Consent and Communications

By submitting your information through this site, you expressly consent to be contacted by a licensed insurance agent or agency via telephone call, text message (SMS), or email regarding insurance products and services. You understand that:

  • Your consent is not a condition of purchasing any insurance product
  • Calls or texts may be made using an automatic telephone dialing system or pre-recorded messages
  • Message and data rates may apply for text messages
  • You may opt out of text messages at any time by replying STOP
  • You may withdraw consent to be contacted at any time by contacting us at the address below

Information Sharing

We may share your information with:

  • Licensed insurance agents and agencies — for the purpose of providing you with a coverage review and quote
  • Service providers — who help us operate our platform, under confidentiality agreements

We do not sell your personal information to third parties for marketing purposes.

Data Retention

We retain your information for as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes described in this policy, or as required by applicable law. You may request deletion at any time by contacting us.

Your Rights

  • Access the personal information we hold about you
  • Request correction of inaccurate information
  • Request deletion of your information
  • Opt out of future communications at any time

Security

We use HTTPS encryption to protect your information in transit. Form submissions are transmitted securely to our lead management system.

Children's Privacy

This service is not directed at individuals under the age of 18. We do not knowingly collect personal information from minors.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time and will post the updated policy with a revised "last updated" date.
